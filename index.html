The Origin Story

01

The Roots

2020

Rewind to 2020. Three friends—Yash, Kavin, and Sachin—found solace in the digital battlegrounds of Free Fire during their 10th grade. It was here, amidst the school stress, that our shared passion took root.

02

The Struggle

2020 - 2021

We started as novices. We faced defeats, balanced academics, and navigated the challenges of being beginners. But we hatched a plan to channel this passion into something extraordinary.

03

The Revival

2021

Upon completing 10th grade, armed with determination, 'MR. Gaming' was officially born. It became a testament to our enduring friendship and unwavering dedication.

04

The Growth

Present

Today, we stand as Grandmasters. 'MR. Gaming' is more than a channel; it's a community where the spirit of gaming meets genuine camaraderie. We are turning our passion into a career.
